Replaces your IDE's progress bars with random Pokémon.

If you like this plugin, please consider rating it on the Marketplace or starring it on Github!

Supported Pokemon

Features

  • Over 100 Pokémon from all 9 generations
  • Multiple colour schemes & styles
  • Replace loading spinners with spinning Pokéball
  • Fully configurable: Preferences > Appearance & Behaviour > Pokémon Progress
    • turn off & on Pokémon sprites
    • turn on and off loading spinner Pokéball
    • change color schemes & paint style
    • enable/disable specific Pokémon or generations
    • change speed of indeterminate progress bar
